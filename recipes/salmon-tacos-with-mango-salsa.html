Salmon Tacos With Mango Salsa

Description

Salmon tacos with a mango salsa. Tastes really good and is nice for meal prep.

Serves 8 and has about 450 calories per serving

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Tortilla
  • Mangos

Steps

  1. Cook salmon
  2. Season salmon
  3. Make mango salsa
  4. Mix salmon and salsa into tortilla
